U.S. Army Spouse Leave Portal

Official military leave application for spouses and families

Authorized Military Leave Assistance

This portal supports verified applications for U.S. Army spouse and relationship-based leave.

Start Application

Requirements

▼
  • Applicant must be the legal spouse or long-term partner of active-duty Army personnel
  • Valid marriage certificate or proof of relationship (photos, communication history, etc.)
  • Military ID or verification of soldier’s active status
  • Brief statement explaining the leave request
  • Note: Non-married partners are accepted for leave support based on commanding officer discretion.

Processing Fee

▼

The official document verification and support fee is $195. This includes:

  • Administrative support
  • Secure document handling
  • Direct liaison with military leave coordinators

Application Steps

▼
  1. Enter personal and soldier details
  2. Upload proof documents (passport, marriage certificate or relationship proof)
  3. Submit request for review
  4. Receive confirmation and processing update via email
